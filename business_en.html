If you want to sell your goods and services in China then you must ensure that you are aware of your rights and responsibilities under Chinese law.

In China, Yike was a licenced lawyer in a reputable law firm before migrating to Australia. Yike provided the full range of legal services to Chinese and overseas companies. Clients Yike served in China included

  • Caltex
  • Chevron
  • ELF Lubricants
  • Singapore Petroleum
  • Honda
  • Holiday Inn, and
  • Bossini.

Yike can assist you with

  • due diligence reports with information drawn largely from official records including ownership of company or business, ownership of real estate property, permitted uses for land and factory premises
  • negotiation with Chinese partners
  • establishing your operations in China
  • ongoing support for your legal services in china

Want to export to Australia?

Competition in the export market is fierce. If you have a potential buyer from Australia, you may have concerns about their credibility.

  • company history
  • company investment relationships

Reports can be provided in Chinese, English or in both languages.
